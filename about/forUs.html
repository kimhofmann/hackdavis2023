We are ForUs

ForUs is a safe space for Queer individuals, grounded in the philosophy of mutual aid. We believe in supporting and uplifting each other through shared resources, knowledge, and experiences. Join our vibrant community to find a network of like-minded individuals who understand your unique journey. Explore our diverse range of resources, from personal stories and educational materials to practical advice and support networks. Together, we foster an inclusive environment where Queer individuals can thrive, find solidarity, and build a stronger, more empowered community. Join us today and embrace the power of mutual aid in your Queer experience.

The people behind ForUs

We are a group of queer, neurodivergent design students in our senior year at UC Davis. We created ForUs as a way to connect with the local LGBTQIA+ community and as a way for the local LGBTQIA+ community to connect with each other.
Our team is comprised of Kim and Brenda (development) & Jenny and Santhoshi (research and design).
